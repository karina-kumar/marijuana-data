Need to change date_arrest to date type. Need to get rid of extra words in race and sex. And fet rid of Case No altogether.

  date_arrest = mdy(date_arrest),
  race = substr(race, 1, 1),
  sex = substr(sex, 1, 1)
) |> select(-"Case No")
